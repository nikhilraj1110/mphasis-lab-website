About the Lab

The Mphasis AI & Applied Tech Lab at Ashoka University advances interdisciplinary research in AI, health, fabrication, and beyond.

Our Mission

The Mphasis AI & Applied Tech Lab at Ashoka University is a collaboration between Ashoka University and Mphasis F1 Foundation, a partnership that has grown stronger since its founding in 2020. The Lab brings together researchers from seven departments — from biology and chemistry to computer science and environmental studies — to tackle problems that no single discipline can solve.

The Lab runs 25+ active research projects across five pillars. BharatSim builds large-scale simulations for epidemic modelling and public health policy. CHART (Centre for Health Analytics Research & Trends) works on clinical AI, food computing, and cancer diagnostics. AI@Ashoka applies AI and machine learning across biology, chemistry, environmental science, education, and media analysis. The Cybersecurity pillar develops post-quantum cryptography protocols for internet security.

The Digital Makerspace is a workshop and fabrication lab where students and researchers design, build, and test their ideas — from laser-cut prototypes to custom scientific instruments. From laser cutters and CNC routers to 3D printers and drones, the Makerspace supports both rapid prototyping and scientific instrumentation for research across multiple pillars.

Structure

Five Research Pillars

Our research is organized into five interconnected pillars, each addressing distinct technological and scientific challenges while sharing infrastructure, talent, and cross-disciplinary insights.

AI@Ashoka

AI@Ashoka

AI and machine learning applied across health, biodiversity, education, policy, and materials science. 14 projects spanning seven departments.

BharatSim

BharatSim

India-scale simulation framework for epidemic modelling and public health policy. Research on H5N1, HIV, typhoid, and mpox covered by BBC, NDTV, Nature India, and 20+ international outlets.

CHART

CHART

Centre for Health Analytics Research and Trends. AI applied to clinical diagnosis, nutrition and food science, and cancer research, in collaboration with hospitals and research institutes.

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity

Post-quantum cryptography research developing encryption protocols that can withstand quantum computing. Published at ACM ASIACCS 2025.

Makerspace

Makerspace

A fabrication lab equipped with laser cutters, 3D printers, CNC machines, and drones. Students and researchers build prototypes, scientific instruments, and hardware projects. Also home to RedBrick Hacks.

History

Our Journey

August 2020
Lab Established

Partnership established between Mphasis F1 Foundation and Ashoka University.

2020 – 2023
Foundation

Research launched with 12 faculty researchers across AI, epidemiological modelling, and cybersecurity. 9 publications. BharatSim simulation framework developed.

2023
Growth

The Lab expanded into five research pillars: AI@Ashoka, BharatSim, CHART, Cybersecurity, and Makerspace. Our Research Interns won 1st place in Privacy-Preserved Federated Learning and 4th place in the SENSORIUM Challenge at NeurIPS 2023.

2023 – 2024
Expansion

Faculty researchers grew from 12 to 19 across 7 departments. 12 new publications across the five pillars.

2024 – 2026
Current

25+ active projects with 25+ faculty researchers across 7 departments. 30+ publications across journals and conferences. 40+ partnerships with institutions in India and internationally.

Highlights

Key Achievements

Some of the milestones that define the Lab's impact — from global media coverage to national recognition.

BharatSim

Global Media Coverage

The BharatSim H5N1 avian influenza study was covered by BBC, NDTV, Nature India, and 20+ international media outlets, bringing the Lab's epidemiological modelling work to a global audience.

AI@Ashoka

Schmidt AI Fellowship at Oxford

Faculty from the Lab were awarded the prestigious Schmidt AI in Science Fellowship at the University of Oxford, recognizing excellence in AI-driven interdisciplinary research.

AI@Ashoka

India-AI Impact Summit Compendium

SocraticAI — an AI tutoring system for foundational CS education — was selected as a case study in MeitY's Compendium on the Real-World Impact of AI in Education.

AI@Ashoka

NeurIPS 2023 Competition Wins

Our Research Interns won 1st place in Privacy-Preserved Federated Learning and 4th place in the SENSORIUM Challenge at NeurIPS 2023.

Cybersecurity

ACM ASIACCS 2025 Publication

Post-quantum cryptography research from the Lab was published at ACM ASIACCS 2025, one of the top venues in computer security.

Makerspace

RedBrick Hacks

The Lab's flagship hackathon series has engaged 430+ participants across multiple editions, fostering innovation and hands-on problem-solving among students.
