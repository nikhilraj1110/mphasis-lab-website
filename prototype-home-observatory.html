Impact

Research That Makes Headlines

From global media coverage to government recognition — our research creates real-world impact.

BharatSim: Global Headlines

H5N1 avian flu modelling study published in BMC Public Health — covered by BBC, NDTV, Nature India, and 20+ international outlets. Platform also deployed to the National AIDS Control Organization for HIV predictive modelling.

Cancer Diagnostics AI

OncoMark — AI framework for quantifying cancer hallmarks across millions of single cells. Published in Communications Biology (Nature portfolio).

Oxford Fellowship

Meghna Agarwala won 3-year Schmidt AI for Science Fellowship at Oxford for her work on AI-driven pollen detection and biodiversity monitoring.

IndiaMediaLens

First national-level tool for summarising and visualising Indian press complaints — 34 years of Press Council of India data, deployed publicly at pressanalytics.in.

RedBrick Hacks III

430+ applicants from 91+ universities across 23 states. 57 finalists competed across four SDG-aligned challenge tracks over 48 hours (February 6–8, 2026).

Government Recognition

SocraticAI — an AI tutoring system for foundational CS education — featured as a case study in MeitY's India-AI Impact Summit Compendium on the Real-World Impact of AI in Education, launched at Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi (February 17, 2026).
